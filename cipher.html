Case Study

Cipher

Your finances, finally made clear with AI

Cipher is a digital-first banking app built around AI. No physical card, just a glass card, crypto wallets, and an AI assistant called Ci that actually learns how you spend. The brand, the app, and the website were all designed from scratch.

The App

Ci handles the thinking

Dark, immersive, with a glass card at the centre. Ci lives inside the app to answer questions, flag subscriptions, and predict cashflow. It adapts to how you actually use money. Everything else is there too: crypto, savings goals, spending limits, instant transfers.

The Website

Introducing Ci

Prototyped in Relume AI to establish the structure, then taken into Figma and finished with Spline interactions. The landing page introduces Ci, walks through the key features, and drives sign-up.

Brand

The mark comes from Ci

Stack a C and an I and you get a Psi. Raleway typeface, electric green on near-black, and a tone that's confident without being corporate.
