Case Study

Captr

Your day, at a glance.

A daily to-do companion designed for the wrist. Capture tasks on the fly throughout your day, wake up to a prioritised list, reorder it in seconds, and tick things off with a satisfying interaction. If something doesn't happen — push it to tomorrow. No friction, no overthinking.

01

Quick capture

Jot a task any time — no categories, no setup. Just text and an optional date. Capture now, sort later.

02

Daily list

Every morning, tasks tagged for today surface as a clean checklist. Your day, assembled overnight.

03

Reorder

Drag tasks to set your priority for the day. The order changes; the colours tell you what matters most.

04

Tick off

A satisfying swipe-to-complete with haptic feedback and a check animation. Small wins should feel like wins.

Priority through colour.

The sunset gradient does the thinking for you. Red means do it now. As urgency drops, the colour shifts through orange and yellow. At a glance — in 3 seconds on a watch screen — you know exactly where to start. Circular action buttons keep the UI clear: rectangles are tasks you swipe, circles are buttons you press.
